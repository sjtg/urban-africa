about us

We are a Lusaka- and Johannesburg-based group of internationally-experienced professionals focused on creating, transacting and managing investment grade property assets.

Urban Africa Real Estate Group was formed in 2015, when our founders saw an opportunity to fill a niche for quality commercial and hospitality focused developments in Zambia.

Since establishing our business, we have successfully delivered a number of development projects, and have a strong pipeline of development project with strong tenants.

To capitalise upon the diverse skillset of our team members, we have recently expanded our offering to provide corporate finance and asset management services for clients on a range of properties across Zambia.

solution

Solutions

With over 80 years of combined experience in African Real Estate, we are able to offer a variety of services to market players in Zambia.

Whilst our main specialism is the development of supermarket-led convenience retail and building supply stores, we have successfully constructed a range of asset classes including hotels, residential and schools.

We have experience in developing and delivering large scale residential projects. We are continuing to expand our development footprint across the country.

We offer commercial investment advisory for Zambian and international investment clients including acquisitions, sales and debt raising.

We also have a strong research capacity, with a track record in delivering extensive market research reports.

Asset and property management of proprietary and related party assets. We have successfully turned around malls, through securing international anchors and line tenants on competitive rental agreements.
